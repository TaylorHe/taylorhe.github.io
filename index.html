CURRENT PROJECTS

RockSat

October 2016 - Present

RockSat is an ongoing project approved by NASA in which I am working with several other engineers and a mechanical engineering professor to place a vibration-free capsule on future NASA payloads. This capsule contains both active and passive vibration isolation. My responsibilities include designing a system to parse the vibrations detected by 8 accelerometers and actively cancel them using a microprocessor. In addition, I am creating software to post-process accelerometer data after the rocket returns and display them in an easy to read format to see if the isolation chamber was successful.
